What is gnawa Music?

I don't wanna get into the history of gnawa music but here is a famous gnawa song that i think is a good introduction to gnawa music.

why i decided to go ?

There is a place called l'uzine in my city that seems to serve as an outlet to young people through Music, graffiti, dancing...etc.
I knew about the place for a long time now and always talked to a friend about visiting it.
This was the perfect opportunity. I went to discover the place, Enjoy gnawa music and just have a good time.

How i almost missed it ?

The person organizing the whole thing(us going to the show) almost bailed on me.
He was supposed to text me the place we'll meet at but never did. 45 minutes before the show, I called him and he answered that he was already on his way to the show.
The plan was that we'll(6 people total) meet at a specific place, 2 of us will go on motorcycles and the rest will uber there,
Turns out, the guy with the motorcycle and 1 other dude canceled last minute which put the organizer in tough situation.
He thought that he was late for the show (and was afraid the show will sold out), He quickly borrowed a motorcycle and took 1 dude with him without telling me (which is when i called).
After i called him, he decided to come back and pick me up and there we go 3 people on 2 people motorcycle hoping the cops won't see us or crash.

How was the show ?

The show was cool af, they played late but not too late for it to be annoying.
I danced most of the time, although i had some anxious thoughts, those thoughts did not bother me much, I danced like i don't give a fuck who's watching me dancing silly
I had a few hits from a spliff which calmed me down even more for a few moments.
Fun stuff !
